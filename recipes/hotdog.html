Steps

  1. De-fucking-frost. Please.
  2. Measure the amount of oil with your guts.
  3. Pre-heat the cooking oil with whatever temperature. Just don't forgetti to sprinkle some water to check if it explodes already.
  4. During pre-heating, put some slits on the sides of each hotdogs.
  5. If the oil is hot enough, cook the hotdogs until the insides are golden brown.
  6. Server hot with banana ketchup or Mang Tomas sauce.
